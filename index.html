Events

01

Event
Boxville

A vehicle is hidden somewhere on the map. You and your teammate get 3 hints to find it, then must deliver it to a destination announced in chat. Other teams do the same and will try to stop you by shooting on sight.

The first team to deliver the vehicle twice wins.



02

Event
Stand your yacht

A PvP event where teams compete to capture the helipad on a yacht by holding it for 40 seconds. Other teams will try to kill you and take over the helipad, while the defending team must protect it for 10 minutes.

Teams that either defend successfully for 10 minutes or achieve 2 captures advance to the semi-final or final.


03

Event
the bullet train

A PvP event where teams must find and board a moving train driven by me, then stay onboard until it reaches a specified destination. Other teams will shoot on sight trying to take over.

The first team to reach two destinations onboard wins. If multiple teams arrive at the destination simultaneously, a new destination is assigned. Only one team can hold the destination at a time.
